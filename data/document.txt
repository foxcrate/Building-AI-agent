Top 10 Most Watched Movies of All Time

1. Avatar (2009)
Director: James Cameron
Genre: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy
Runtime: 162 minutes
Worldwide Box Office: Over $2.9 billion

Overview:
Avatar is a visually groundbreaking science fiction epic that redefined modern filmmaking. It introduced revolutionary 3D technology and immersive world-building, setting new standards for visual effects.

Plot Summary:
Jake Sully, a former Marine, travels to the alien moon Pandora as part of the Avatar Program. He becomes deeply connected to the native Na'vi people and ultimately leads them in a battle to protect their world from human exploitation.

Cultural Impact:
- Revolutionized 3D cinema
- Set new standards for visual effects
- Inspired discussions on environmentalism

Legacy:
Remains the highest-grossing movie of all time and launched a major film franchise.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Avengers: Endgame (2019)
Director: Anthony & Joe Russo
Genre: Action, Adventure, Superhero
Runtime: 181 minutes
Worldwide Box Office: Over $2.79 billion

Overview:
Avengers: Endgame is the epic conclusion to Marvel’s Infinity Saga, bringing together over a decade of interconnected storytelling.

Plot Summary:
After Thanos wipes out half of all life, the Avengers attempt a time-travel mission to undo the damage, leading to emotional sacrifices and a massive final battle.

Cultural Impact:
- Record-breaking opening weekend
- Huge global fan engagement

Legacy:
Defined a new standard for cinematic universes.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Titanic (1997)
Director: James Cameron
Genre: Romance, Drama, Historical
Runtime: 195 minutes
Worldwide Box Office: Over $2.2 billion

Overview:
A tragic love story set against the sinking of the RMS Titanic.

Plot Summary:
Jack and Rose fall in love aboard the Titanic, only to face heartbreak when the ship sinks.

Legacy:
Won 11 Academy Awards and became one of the most beloved films of all time.
